Privacy Policy

Yeah Buddy iOS App

Privacy Overview

We take your privacy seriously. The "Yeah Buddy" application is designed to respect your personal data and provide a secure, private experience while you workout.

No Data Collection

The "Yeah Buddy" app does NOT collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software. Your name, email, location, and workout data remain entirely on your device.

Microphone Usage

The app requests access to your device's microphone. This input is used locally on your device for the sole purpose of voice detection (to count reps or provide auditory feedback).

No audio is recorded, stored, or sent off-device. The processing happens in real-time and the audio data is discarded immediately after processing.
